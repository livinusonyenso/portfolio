    The Blockchain – helping us help you. In 2023, ChainAnalysis estimated that illicit cryptocurrency addresses received a total of $24.2 billion. With new technologies, present new opportunities for wealth creation because of this, there is an increase in scammers exploiting unsuspecting individuals. Blockchain technology is set to transform various sectors, including supply chain management, law, finance, government, and the public sector. A blockchain is a public ledger and it records every transaction that occurs on it. We’ve observed a significant rise in fraudulent investments where scammers persuade clients to pay in cryptocurrency. Blockchain always records every transaction. This creates a digital footprint that we can follow to potentially track down the end destination and take action towards recovery. WRS has heavily invested in a team of cryptocurrency specialists. Our team uses tools employed by the FBI and NCA, giving us access to a vast database of information exclusive to law enforcement and high tracing accuracy. This enables us to assist clients who have fallen victim to fraud, as well as work with insolvency practitioners, family lawyers, and forensic accountants in tracing crypto assets.
